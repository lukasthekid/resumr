# Resumr Brand Value Document

Reference document for the Resumr brand. Use this when redesigning the landing page or making design decisions to stay consistent with brand identity.

---

## 1. Product Identity

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Name** | Resumr |
| **Logo treatment** | "Resum" in slate-900, "r" in indigo |
| **Tagline / Core promise** | Stop sending generic applications. Get hired faster. |
| **One-liner** | AI-powered resume and cover letter tailoring for every job description—in seconds. |

---

## 2. What the App Does

- **Tailor profile in seconds** — AI analyzes job descriptions and highlights your most relevant experience instantly.
- **ATS-Friendly, Human-Readable** — Generate perfectly formatted PDFs that pass the bots and impress hiring managers.
- **Import any job in one click** — Paste links from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and more; we parse and extract key details automatically.
- **Organize your job search** — Centralized dashboard with visual pipeline, status tracking, notes, and reminders (no more spreadsheets).
